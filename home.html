RIGHTS FOR PETS:

Having a pet, mainly a dog or cat, brings a lot of responsibility.Under this law, animal owners are under an obligation to provide and promote their pets well-being. More specifically, pets should: have a suitable environment to live in, have a suitable diet, be able to exhibit normal behaviour patterns, have any need it has to be housed with, or apart from, other animals, be protected from pain, suffering, injury and disease.
